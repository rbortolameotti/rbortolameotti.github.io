Research

I am a computer science PhD student at the University of Twente working under the supervision of Andreas Peter. I am broadly interested in several aspects of security and privacy, such as applied cryptography, distributed systems security, network security and machine learning. Currently I am particularly interested to the security and privacy challenges related to data leakage/exfiltration.
